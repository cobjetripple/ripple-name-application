Ripple Name Application

Applying for a name and completing the payment does not guarantee that the Ripple name will be claimable.

Applications must show some previous or intended use of the name, which can include, but is not limited to:

  • Having a registered business, or doing-business-as, with that name.
  • Owning and controling a related domain name.
  • Not infringing on known trademarks or service marks.
  • Not intended to confuse or mislead.

Applications will be reviewed in early May. Ripple names not on the reserved list, including names pending application review, will be available for claiming in the next few weeks.

Reserved list:

  • The most visited 100,000 domains
  • All 1-character and 2-character names
  • Current [name]@ripple.com addresses

Application Fee

Each application requires a 10,000XRP fee. The fee goes to a black hole address. Ripple Labs does not collect this fee.
